INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. 1. Choose the difficulty level and click the "START GAME" button to start the game.

  2. 2. Depending on the difficulty level you will see different size sets of cards.

  3. 3. Each card has a matching pair somewhere, both of them have the same picture.

  4. 4. You will have 10 seconds to memorize where are all the matching pairs located.

  5. 5. When you see all cards face-down you can start opening matching pairs by clicking on cards.

  6. 6. If you clicked on two cards and they match, those cards will stay face up.

  7. 7. If they didn't match they will flip face down after 0.5 seconds.

  8. 8. Your goal is to open all the matching pairs, and your score is the number of clicks you used.

  9. 9. The highest possible score is equal to the amount of cards you will see.
